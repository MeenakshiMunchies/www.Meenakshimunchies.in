Why Meenakshi's Munchies?

MEENAKSHI'S MUNCHIES – TRADITION IN EVERY BITE! Introducing Meenakshi's Munchies, a passion-driven Indian food start-up founded by Neeta Susmita Sinha, a hardworking mother entrepreneur with a vision to bring healthy, affordable, and traditional flavors to every home. With a deep love for India’s rich culinary heritage, she is on a mission to revive lost recipes and make authentic, homemade delights accessible to all. Inspired by the concept of 'Annapurna', the goddess of nourishment, Meenakshi's Munchies is dedicated to offering food that not only delights the taste buds but also nourishes the soul. Each recipe is a tribute to the warmth and wisdom of Indian kitchens, where tradition meets wellness. Currently serving Bengaluru, we offer a range of healthy and happy snacking options: 🍪 Thekua (Bihari Cookies) – A crunchy, wholesome snack perfect for mid-daycravings. 🌿 Ayurvedic Gond Gud Laddoo – A powerhouse of immunity-boosting goodness. 🌾 Flax Seed Laddoo – Rich in fiber, nutrition-packed, and great for digestion. 🥟 Gujiya/Pedakiya & Nimki – Classic festive treats, bringing joy in every bite.
